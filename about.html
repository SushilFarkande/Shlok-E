About Us

Your Trusted Laundry Partner

At Shlok Laundry Solutions, we are committed to delivering complete laundry care solutions for homes, hotels, hospitals, laundromats, commercial laundry units, and industrial facilities. With a strong focus on quality, performance, and customer satisfaction, we provide a wide range of premium laundry chemicals, cleaning agents, and professional machine services designed to improve washing efficiency and fabric care.

Our product range includes high-performance detergent liquids, detergent powders, fabric softeners, stain removers, emulsifiers, bleach solutions, glass cleaners, floor cleaners, perfume additives, and specialty laundry chemicals formulated for powerful cleaning and long-lasting freshness. Every product is carefully developed to provide effective stain removal, color protection, fabric softness, and hygienic cleaning results while maintaining machine safety and efficiency.

In addition to chemicals, we specialize in complete washing machine and dryer support services. Our experienced and certified technicians handle installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair services for commercial and domestic laundry machines of all major brands. Whether it is routine servicing, machine setup, spare part replacement, or emergency repair work, we ensure fast and reliable solutions that minimize downtime and keep your operations running smoothly.

Why customers choose us:

  • Premium-quality laundry chemicals and accessories
  • Customized solutions for commercial and industrial laundry needs
  • Fast response and reliable machine servicing
  • Expert technicians with technical experience
  • Affordable pricing with dependable support
  • Long-lasting cleaning performance and fabric care
  • Trusted service for hotels, hospitals, laundries, and businesses

At Shlok Laundry Solutions, our mission is to provide advanced laundry solutions that combine quality products, professional service, and customer trust under one reliable brand.
